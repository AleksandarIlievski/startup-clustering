Creating, automating and optimising direct mail has never been easier 
 
1
Choose delivery country, set triggers and map data fields
Define where, when and which data you use for recurring direct mail campaigns. For example, activate one-time shoppers, cross-sell or reactivate inactive customers.
 
2
Pick the perfect format and print method
Configure your campaigns and choose from the best-performing formats and modern print methods. Then lean back - we manage printing and delivery across Europe.
 
3
Upload designs for any customer segment
Personalise your direct mail in any possible way: A/B test, target buyer personas via individual designs and even benefit from 1:1 picture personalization.
 
4
Add direct mail touchpoints to your journeys
Close the loop and add offline touchpoints to your 
existing online customer journeys within a few clicks - 
to make true cross-channel customer journeys a reality.
