ILOCATOR INFRASTRUCTURE MANAGEMENT SOLUTION
Automated, continuous and unbiased data acquisition. An artificial intelligence powered data refinery. And a collaboration platform.
 
01
Sensor technology
Optical / Physical / Customized
3D imaging, aerial imaging, movement, vibration and location sensors. Our proprietary sensor technology and software ecosystem is what makes iLocator unique. If there is no technology out there to solve your specific problem, we can create a fitting sensor from scratch. 
02
Data capture
Automated / Continuous / Unbiased
Data is the foundation for managing infrastructure. With iLocator, data capture is a background process through vehicle deployed sensors. No additional effort for the workforce on the ground is our focus, but if it is necessary there are options for manual data capture as well. 
03
Business intelligence
Analysis / Patterns / Insights
Turn your data into valuable information. Our technology provides historical, current, and predictive views of your infrastructure operations. 
04
Integrations
Ready made / Tailor made.
No silos – iLocator is an open platform. Benefit from built-in integrations to 3rd party geospatial platforms and task management solutions. Or let us customize an integration to fit your needs. 
05
Strategic planning
Decision making / Management / Team work
Your Infrastructure has tremendous strategic value. Insights into your infrastructure combined with tools for teamwork and management improve your decision-making and help you capitalize on your infrastructure’s value over time. 
HOW YOU BENEFIT
Eliminate separate data acquisition processes. Get an immediate overview of your infrastructure. Reduce driving by up to 10%. Contribute to carbon reduction and improve sustainability. Save up to 30% on your maintenance work.
