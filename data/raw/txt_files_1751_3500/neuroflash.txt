How are the number of words calculated? What happens to my unused words?
You only pay for the generated, visible words. 
In the Basic Plan we credit your words for the next month. So you never lose unused words.
In the Free Plan you really have 2,000 words free every month, no matter if you wrote less in the previous month.
How is neuroflash different?
Just like our competitors, we generate short- and long-form marketing copy. We do this with exceptionally high quality in German and English. Additionally, neuroflash adds a worldwide unique text analysis on top of the text generation. Our AI gives you a prediction of what people will think and feel when they read your copy. With the prediction score, you are able to increase your marketing ROI and brand activation by making the right content decisions. Additionally, we offer a free plan, with which you can write 2000 words every month for free!
Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
No. neuroflash is a pay as you go monthly subscription service.
Is the content new every time & plagiarism free?
Yes, our AI generates unique content every time by using bits and pieces of words from the internet. It is trained to not use previously written sentences as it can detect patterns in phrases, but to rather change sentences and to keep the same meaning. The content is accordingly free of plagiarism.
What is included in the free plan & do I have to add my credit card?
No, you just create your account and can start right away. neuroflash free plan includes access to all writing features, except of the text validation tool, image search ad SEO analysis which is part of the Power and Premium plan. With the free plan you can write up to 2000 words for free every month. You can review the full list of features for each of the plans on our pricing page.
Which technology stands behind neuroflash?
GPT-3 is an autoregressive language model developed and launched by OpenAI. It is based on a gigantic neural network with 175 million synapses and can add and compose texts by itself under minimal specifications. It is the largest Natural Language Processing Transformer (NLP) that can accurately and effectively reproduce human thought and reasoning patterns. The generated texts are so well composed that readers can no longer distinguish them from texts written by humans. Since GPT-3 was launched in 2020, it generates an average of 4.5 million words a day and is used by more than 300 apps.
In what language does neuroflash generate copy?
neuroflash is currently able to produce content in German, English, Italian, Spanish, French, Polish and Dutch. Stay tuned, for more languages, though!
What is the difference between AI Writer & AI Tester?
With the AI Writer you are able to create short- and long-form copy. You have access to a very intuitive editor and a powerful sidebar, which connects your creation process with the ideation & research phase.
The AI Tester lets you compare and test short-form copy with our Flash score, the unique prediction score which tells you what people will think and feel when they ready your copy. In other words: with the AI Tester you can optimize your copy.
Will my data be private and safe?
We take data security seriously at neuroflash. Our servers are hosted in a world-class data center that is protected by 24-hour surveillance and we ensure that our application is always up-to-date with the latest security checks. Our data center is also EU compliant and all our plans include SSL encryption to keep your data safe.
