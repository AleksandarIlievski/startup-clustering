WHY?
We love Virtual Reality and Multimedia because it makes fun, saves time and CO2 and offers unlimited possibilities.
HOW?
The intuitive and immersive usage of real space, interaction and information is the key to simplify communication and learning.
WHAT?
Creation, sharing and collaboration in VR should be as easy as possible. Therefore we have developed the VR-Suite, Workspace-VR  and offer great VR projects.
We want to enable everyone to create and use VR.
