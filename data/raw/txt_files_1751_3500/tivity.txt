Object-/Class-Designer
Create Your Datamodel
 
 
Definition of data sources for the usage on the platform
Creation of objects, attributes and actions
Definition of attribute types   and their controls
Linking between objects and look-ups (with parameters) 
Translations of names   and help descriptions
 
 
Benefits
Visualization-based creating, editing and examining of object
Enables standard views of objects without defining layouts
Templates simplify the definition of data sources
Validation of inputs to ensure accuracy
 
 
Selection-Designer
Build Result-Lists & Reports
 
 
Creation of queries for layouts and reports
Definition of output data fields and query parameters
Preview for queries
Translations of the title and field names
Authorization management to control execution of queries
 
 
Benefits
Simple visualization of query parameters operators and groups
Filter results for reports and diagrams
No SQL-knowledge required
Validation of inputs to ensure accuracy
 
 
Layout-Designer
Configure Layouts & Dashboards
 
 
Creation of customized user  interfaces for apps
 Customizing data fields and actions
Templates  for frequently used layouts 
Inheritance of layouts for layouts and rights
Supports adaptive layouts for different screen resolutions
 
 
Benefits
WYSIWYG editor
Extensive set of layout elements and controls
No HTML knowledge necessary (drag&drop editor)
Preview to quickly validate solutions
Reuse of layouts through inheritance (of basis classes)
Includes functions for help and introductional tours
 
 
Workflow-/Script-Designer
Define Business-Logic with Workflows & Scripts
 
 
Definition of workflows and processes
Multiple steps and transitions between them are possible
Actions within steps  and transitions 
Parameters for the start and runtime of the workflow
Authorization management for executing workflows
 
 
Benefits
Visualization of steps and transitions for easy validation
Easy handling of repeatable tasks (e.g. invoicing, E-Mail orders)
No development and coding knowledge required (drag&drop)
Validation of inputs to ensure accuracy
 
 
Template-Designer
Generate Documents & E-Mails with Templates
 
 
Definition of workflows and processes
Multiple steps and transitions between them are possible
Actions within steps  and transitions 
Parameters for the start and runtime of the workflow
Authorization management for executing workflows
 
 
Benefits
WYSIWYG creation of documents and E-Mails
No 3rd party software necessary (Creation directly in a browser without MS-Office or similar programs)
No HTML knowledge necessary (easy to use HTML editor)
Reuse of templates through nesting and sections
