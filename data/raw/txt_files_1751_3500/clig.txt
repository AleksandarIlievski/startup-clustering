City of London Investment Group PLC
City of London Investment Group PLC (CLIG) is an established asset management group listed on the London Stock Exchange, consisting of two wholly-owned subsidiaries that invest primarily in closed-end funds for the benefit of their respective clients:
City of London Investment Management Company Limited (CLIM), which historically specialized in Emerging Markets, but now has expanded its range to include International, Opportunistic Value, Frontier, and Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) strategies, for primarily institutional clients.
Learn more about CLIM
Karpus Investment Management (KIM), which provides closed-end fund strategies across all asset classes to wealth management clients in the United States.
Learn more about KIM
The Group exists for the mutual benefit of our three primary stakeholders: Clients, Employees, and Shareholders
