Download to discover! 
 
 
  
Giving backJustPlay enables players around the world to be rewarded daily for the time they invest in playing games they love. We consider this a new paradigm in digital entertainment and our part in trying to build a more fair world. More importantly we also believe that every human wants to help make the world a better place. So we had an idea. Let’s allow players to donate their earnings to a charity of their choice and we will - naturally - match every dollar they give. We decided on a list of causes that we believe every human in the world will be passionate about: Climate Change, Hunger and Health. After substantial research we settled on three charities for players to donate to, where not only we believe every dollar donated will be the most impactful, but also where players can learn the most about each cause. For climate change we selected the Clean Air Task Force, for hunger The Hunger Project and for health Doctors without Borders.Now players will have the opportunity to play and do good at the same time. We think that's something pretty awesome.
