About 3a
 
 
 
 
Vision 3a
 
3a-diagnostics GmbH is a start-up located in the Southwest of Germany dedicated to the development of Point of Care (POC) test systems that can be applied without any logistical effort, without training, without analyzers and energy supply – by anyone, anywhere and anytime.
 
 
 
 
 
Anyone, Anywhere, Anytime
 
 
 
 
Taste Your Health
 
The human tongue has basic taste areas to detect sweet, salty, sour, bitter and umami. 3a Biosensors use the tongue with its bitter taste areas as a 24/7 indicator of the health status of the oral cavity.
 
 
 
 
 
Our Strength
 
Our team of experienced scientists from different disciplines with many years of work experience in the field of the development, production set-up, automation, and sales of medical devices is a strong partner in developing products adapted to customers’ wishes.
 
Contact Us
 
 
Quality
 
Although 3a is a young start-up in the medical device industry. Hence, development, production and sales are compliant with the regulations for In-vitro Diagnostics in the European Union.
