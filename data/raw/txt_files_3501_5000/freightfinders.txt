Comparing freight forwarders & finding cheap transport prices
 
Transport & freight costs calculator
Our freight calculator at the top of the page combines sea freight, air freight, rail and truck transport and thus finds the ideal logistics company for your route. The prices of all forwarding agents within our network are compared and the best offer is selected.
  
Freight forwarders auction-tool
Should we not be able to display an immediate price, we will open an exclusive bidding process for you. This way, all eligible freight forwarders can make bids on your transport. You can reach all partner carriers with one click and receive an email after each offer to keep you informed. 
  
Freight forwarders’ and logistics services
Find everything you are looking for in the logistics area at Freightfinders. We offer countless services from reliable forwarding agents for cheap and easy transports of your goods. Find the right transport company online in a minimum of time and book additional services such as customs clearance, transport insurance, packing & loading.

Sea freight - transports with Freightfinders
Today more than ever, businesses are global, and the trend continues. Shipping sea freight to Hong Kong today and next month a sea transport from New York. 
Freightfinders offers the possibility to bring your goods safely and quickly to your destination with the help of trained logistics specialists. Let experts help you benefit from fair prices and reliable sea freight forwarders. Blind trust was yesterday – with Freightfinders, you take control of your goods flows, reduce transport and freight costs and receive targeted information about your sales markets.
  
Shipping FCL & LCL sea freight from /to Germany
Whether you have a pallet of spare parts from Hamburg to Shanghai, or a 40ft. high cube container from Taiwan to Berlin, we offer to help you with any sea freight. Freightfinders has transport prices from over 300 ports worldwide from Germany (export) and to Germany (import). You want to ship containers? Compare sea freight prices and use the Freightfinders dashboard for free with GPS monitoring of all your container transports.

Air freight - transports with Freightfinders
Freightfinders is your online transport company for air freight. Find qualified air freight forwarding and additional services. Obtaining offers from countless transport companies is a matter of the past, with Freightfinders you can reach all our partner carriers with just a few clicks.
  
Cheap air freight prices with our bidding tool
Freightfinders offers you the possibility to have air freight forwarders bid on your transport. Start an auction and we will inform you about the best offer. Since we always look for the cheapest quotes, you are usually offered a sea freight price. Simply click on "request air freight price" and let the forwarding agents make bids on your transport request.
Just use the transport comparison at the top of the page.
  
Air freight insurance
In spite of all precautions, there may, unfortunately, be damage to air freight transports, as there can be with any type of transport. It is not necessarily only your goods that need to be affected, so that you have to assume liability. In order not to experience a nasty surprise and to save more costs, we strongly recommend covering air freight insurance, which can simply be done on our platform.

Rail transport with Freightfinders
Rail transport is not only an environmentally friendly alternative, but also a fast and reliable one. With Freightfinders, you can book this type of transport cheaply and quickly and also benefit from many years of experience.
  
Cheap rail freight prices thanks to our auction-tool
At Freightfinders you are no longer dependent on spending your time getting and evaluating offers. Let our partner carriers provide you with your transport request and reach reliable shipping companies with just a few clicks. You can quickly and easily compare the prices of the various transport routes and book a cheap route for your rail transport. Benefit from our experience and the experience of our partners and transport your goods quickly and safely.
  
Container transport via train
FCL via rail transport
We offer you the possibility to transport your rail freight in a FCL container. No matter which goods you want to transport, in this container type anything can be shipped and is additionally well protected. 
 
LCL via freight train transport
LCL containers are the cheaper alternative for your rail transport. However, because of the handling. it is also a more time-consuming option.

Truck transport with Freightfinders
Whether short or long distances, national or international – truck transports are often booked not only separately, but also in combination with other transport modes to transport goods to their destination. Therefore, ensure the best price for your next transport at Freightfinders.com. 
With us, you will find shipping companies that execute your transport at the best prices.
 
Cheap truck transport prices thanks to our auction-tool
With Freightfinders, you have the possibility to start an auction. Let freight forwarders bid on your transport request and find cheap prices. Simply use the freight calculator at the top of the page. If you want to transport goods by sea, you can also arrange a pre-carriage and on-carriage by truck via our website.
 
Container transport via truck
FCL for truck transports
We offer you the option to transport your goods in a FCL truck container. Thus, you have enough space for your goods and can shield them from external influences, as well.
 
LCL for truck transports
With LCL containers, you can save on your truck transport costs. Here, your goods are loaded together with goods of others in one container. This, however, takes more time as the cargo has to be handled.
 
Truck transport

Customs clearance by experienced customs brokers
Because of the increasing international trade, customs clearance is also becoming more and more important. However, due to various agreements, details and exceptions, this task is becoming more and more difficult to manage. In order to facilitate transport and customs clearance, we offer you the services of experienced customs agencies.
 
Why choosing customs clearance with Freightfinders
Errors in customs clearance usually cost not only time but also money. That is why it is particularly important to declare goods very thoroughly. Freightfinders, therefore, works with experienced customs agents who take full care of all aspects of the customs clearance of your transport. Leave the document creation for your transport to our experienced professionals and, thus, make sure that your goods are properly cleared. 
 
Booking customs clearance as an additional service
Find cheap forwarding agents, be it sea freight, air freight, rail or truck transport, and add customs clearance as an additional service. Our customs agents can help you with any customs procedure, no matter which country you want to import from or export to. 
Just use the transport comparison at the beginning of the page.

Car transport
With the Freightfinders freight calculator, you can quickly find and book cheap freight forwarders for your car transport. You want to ship the vehicle to another continent? No problem! We transport your car safely and quickly to your requested destination.
 
Container und RoRo ship transport
So that you can individualise your vehicle transport, you will find the possibility to ship your car, motorbike, etc. by container or RoRo ship at Freightfinders.com. Vehicle transports via container not only offer protection against external influences, but also the choice between a LCL and FCL container. The RoRo ship simplifies the transport of your car and ensures a particularly cheap shipping.
Choose the right offer for you and your vehicle and benefit from experienced shipping companies as well as fast and safe vehicle transport.
 
Price comparison for vehicle transports
Receive direct offers for your vehicle shipping with our forwarding comparison-tool. Should we not be able to offer an immediate price, we will open a free transport auction for you. Then, all partners can bid on your car transport and you will get an ideal partner at a reasonable price.
