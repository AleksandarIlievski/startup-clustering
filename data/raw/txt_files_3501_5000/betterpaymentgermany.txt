One of the most comprehensive online payment processings in Germany
Our services for companies of all types and sizes consist not only of classic online payment, but also of services such as our decision engine for fraud prevention. In addition, we offer downstream services such as individualised payment pages (JetztZahlen) and a white label solution that allows you to become a payment service gateway yourself.

Our ServicesGerman payment technology adapted to your needs under consideration of highest security standards. Our offer includes customizable and flexible solutions for online and offline payments. Profit from our experience in e-commerce and FinTech to increase the turnover of your business.
Online & Offline Payment
Accept more than eight of the most popular online payment methods. In addition we integrate payments from stationary retail via POS terminal into the Better Payment Gateway, so you have everything at a glance - online & offline.
White Label
Become a payment service provider yourself with a dedicated gateway instance in your own look and feel. We make it easier for you to enter the new business field and find a suitable solution for all individual requirements.
JetztZahlen
Reduce the number of outstanding payments. With JetztZahlen you can offer your customers the most convenient solution for online payment of invoices, overdue bills  or donations via customizable payment pages.
Further ServicesAlongside our payment solutions, we provide you with additional services.
Risk Management
Minimize payment defaults by integrating our comprehensive fraud protection using risk & score checks
Receivables Management
Reduce process costs through tailored receivables management solutions for collection and factoring
Subscription Payments
Control recurring payments from your subscription customers with maximum flexibility
Platform Payments
Online payment for platform users and end customers with simple processes in compliant payment models
