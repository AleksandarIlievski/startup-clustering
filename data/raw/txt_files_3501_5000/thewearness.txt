              COCO-MAT ECO RESIDENCES - ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY HOTEL
            
 
Fernweh!Finally we can dream of travelling again. How about a short holiday in Greece - in this environmentally friendly hotel: The Coco-Mat Eco Residences.
A small idyll on the southwestern coast of the picturesque island of Serifos, directly on a sandy beach with a view over the turquoise sea. In former times the workers of the local iron ore mine were accommodated in the building complex. A few years ago, the house was carefully renovated by Greek star architect George Zafiriou without changing the actual character of the complex. Each of the two-story apartments is bright, spacious and furnished in the simple style typical of the country, all have a terrace and a dream view of the Aegean Sea.

SUSTAINABLE FASHION MEETS DESIGN & QUALITY
THE WEARNESS is an online marketplace for sustainable clothing, accessories and beauty. We offer fashion from designer brands that take responsibility and run their business with respect for people and the planet. Find a unique and hand-picked selection of fair fashion and accessories for women and men with timeless designs and impeccable qualities - while preserving the environment or promoting traditional craftsmanship locally. Discover authentic and innovative products - vegan, fair made, upcycled, handcrafted or biodegradable. Each product is tagged with our sustainability icons - so you can easily understand not only what characteristics your favourite product has, but also what brand supports fair working conditions or produces in a regionally and environmentally friendly way. Our symbols are: charitable, eco-friendly, fair, handcrafted, made in Europe, organic, recycled, vegan, female empowerment and zero waste. 
The wearness wants to change the current perception of ethical fashion. We want to offer fashion-conscious customers a positive shopping alternative, without sacrificing style and quality.
