Free to use
Both the registration and the use of the Danagi marketplace are free of charge for you. You can add, edit and purchase an unlimited number of products without incurring any costs.
Discounts & Conditions
As a trader at Danagi, you enjoy special conditions and discounts. With the low commission of only 4% of the net value of the goods, we encourage Danagi sellers to pass the discount on to the buyer. In addition, as a corporate customer, you can use the graduated prices when purchasing your goods from us. These depend on the purchase quantity and the selected models.
Growth & Profitability
Sales are the lifeblood of your company. With Danagi you ensure profitability and the potential for further growth. At the same time, you take action and prevent your team from feeling overwhelmed. They increase the number of units sold, reduce customer acquisition costs and the loss rate compared to competitors and at the same time increase profit margins.
