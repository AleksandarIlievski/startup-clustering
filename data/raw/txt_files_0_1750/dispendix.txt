Automated Liquid Handling Solutions for Laboratories
DISPENDIX is a premier provider of automated liquid handling instruments in the nano to microliter range. Our cutting-edge hardware and software technology enables new discoveries in areas such as drug development, diagnostics, and synthetic biology.
We believe that technological advances and science are made for each other. Good technology simplifies science.
